Professional Overview

Profesyonel Bakış

As an accomplished lawyer, mediator, and consultant with over eight years of experience, I have built a robust legal career characterized by a deep interdisciplinary approach. As the founder and CEO of Guven Istanbul, a pioneering digital earthquake safety platform, I combine legal expertise with technological innovation to enhance community resilience. My legal consultancy spans real estate disputes, urban transformation, and public and administrative law, with a specialization in mediation certified by the Ministry of Justice across various domains including construction, labor, and commercial law.
